Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled whith your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filings:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instruction

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a flutter texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling.Pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evently coat the surface.
  4. add fillinas(optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and servve:As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute, then slide it into a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
